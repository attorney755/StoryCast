Mission statement

"Accessibility is not a feature, it's a right."

At StoryCast, we believe that human stories belong to everyone. Our commitment to accessibility goes beyond compliance — it's about crafting an inclusive digital space where every listener, regardless of ability, can immerse themselves in the world of long-form narrative.

We strive to pioneer a future where voice and technology unite to remove every obstacle between a story and its audience.

Accessibility Features

Captions & Transcripts

High-fidelity, synchronized captions for every piece of audio and video content. Full text transcripts always available to download.

Keyboard Navigation

Every element on StoryCast is fully reachable via keyboard alone. Visible focus indicators always present so you never lose your place.

Screen Reader Support

Semantic HTML5 and ARIA labels optimized for industry-leading screen reading technology, ensuring a rich navigable experience.
